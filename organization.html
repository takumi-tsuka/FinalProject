Mission

Exciting the world by exploring the amazing experience through Udon that instinct delights. An organization that opens up the future through unpredictable evolution.

In order to provide “food richness” to everyone around the world, we will work closely with our customers, employees, and local residents, and strive for communication and cooperation. By doing so, we will continue to create new value and excitement, aiming to realize a sustainable society.

We are an non-profit organization. Profit from events, lesson will be donated.

Event

We will hold many charity events to let people be more familiar with Udon. Let's bring your friends and family and have a good time!

Arrangement recipe

Here are some our original recipe to check out. It is easy for everyone to make. Let's make your special Udon!

  • Ingredients
  • Flozen Udon 200g
  • Egg yoke 1
  • Green Onion 10g
  • Sesami 2 table spoon
  • Seaweed small portion
  • soysause 1 table spoon
  • Sesami oil 2 table spoon
  • How to make
  • 1.Heat the noodle in the oven
  • 2.Mix soysause and sesami oil
  • 3.Put soysause and sesamioil in a bowl, add udon and mix well.
  • 4.Drain the egg yolk, top with green onions, sprinkle with toasted white sesame seeds and chopped seaweed, and if desired, drizzle with chili oil and enjoy.

  • Ingredients
  • Flozen Udon 200g
  • Pork 60g
  • A pinch of sault
  • A pinch of pepper
  • Cabbege 80g
  • Onion 30g
  • Pepper 1
  • Dashi 1 table spoon
  • Soysause 2 table spoon
  • How to make
  • 1.Boil the udon noodles in hot water and drain them in a colander.
  • 2.Cut the pork into 3 cm wide pieces and sprinkle with A.
  • 3.Cut the cabbage into strips 4cm long and 1cm wide, and cut the onion into thin slices vertically. Cut the green pepper into 1 cm wide strips.
  • 4.Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a frying pan, add the pork from (2) and fry. When the color of the meat changes, add the onions, green peppers, and cabbage from (3) in order and stir-fry.
  • 5.Heat 1/3 tbsp of oil in a frying pan, add the udon from (1) and fry. Turn off the heat, add 1/2 teaspoon of Hondashi and soy sauce, mix, turn on the heat, and fry until fragrant over medium heat.
  • 6.Put the removed (4) back in, add 1/2 teaspoon of Hondashi, and stir-fry quickly.

  • Ingredients
  • Boiled Udon 200g
  • 2 slices of Bacon
  • Mushroom 10g
  • Milk 100cc
  • Egg 1
  • Powder cheese 2 table spoon
  • Glined pepper
  • How to make
  • 1.Cut the bacon into bite-sized pieces. Combine cheese and egg mix well. Heat olive oil and garlic in a frying pan.
  • 2.When the aroma comes out, add the bacon and shimeji mushrooms and fry. When the fire goes through, add milk and adjust the taste with salt and pepper.
  • 3.Wash the udon quickly with water to remove the sliminess, drain the water, and put it in the frying pan.
  • 4.Mix Egg and cheese until it reaches the desired firmness. Sprinkle black pepper on the bowl and you're done!
